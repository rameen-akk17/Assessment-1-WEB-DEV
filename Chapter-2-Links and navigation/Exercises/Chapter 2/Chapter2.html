Overview

In 2003, a parasitic fungal infection ravaged the planet, turning humans into violent creatures known as the Infected. Twenty years later, hardened survivor Joel (Pedro Pascal) is hired to smuggle 14-year-old Ellie (Bella Ramsey) to the rebel Fireflies. Season 2 of The Last of Us picks up five years after the events of Season 1. Joel and Ellie’s collective past catches up to them, drawing them into conflict with each other and a world even more dangerous and unpredictable than the one they left behind.

Writers and executive producers, Craig Mazin & Neil Druckmann; executive producers, Carolyn Strauss, Jacqueline Lesko, Cecil O’Connor, Asad Qizilbash, Carter Swan, and Evan Wells. Production companies: PlayStation Productions, Word Games, Mighty Mint, and Naughty Dog.


Characters

  • Joel Miller (Pedro Pascal) - A grizzled survivor with a dark past, tasked with smuggling Ellie.
  • Ellie (Bella Ramsey) - A brave 14-year-old girl immune to the infection, carrying the hope of humanity.
  • Marlene (Merle Dandridge) - The leader of the Fireflies, fighting for a cure.
  • Tommy Miller (Gabriel Luna) - Joel’s younger brother, searching for a new way to live.
  • Bill (Nick Offerman) - A hardened survivor with a distrust of strangers.

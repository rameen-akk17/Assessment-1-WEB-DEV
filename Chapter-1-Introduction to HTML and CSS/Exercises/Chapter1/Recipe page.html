a recipe for pancakes

these classic pancakes are light, fluffy, and easy to make with just a few basic ingredients. perfect for breakfast or brunch, they pair beautifully with butter, maple syrup, fresh fruit, or honey.

ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk (or buttermilk for extra fluffiness)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

method of cooking

  1. mix dry ingredients - in a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. combine wet ingredients - in another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter (or oil), and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. make the batter - gradually pour wet ingredients into dry, stirring gently. do not overmix.
  4. preheat the pan - heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease.
  5. cook the pancakes - pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. flip & finish - cook until golden brown.
  7. serve & enjoy - stack pancakes and serve warm with desired toppings.
